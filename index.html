Getting Started

1

Download the Extension

Click the "Install Extension" button below to download the extension zip file. Then extract it to a folder on your computer.

2

Enable the Extension

Extract the downloaded zip file to a folder. Open Chrome/Edge, navigate to chrome://extensions/, enable "Developer mode", click "Load unpacked", and select the extracted extension folder.

3

Start Shopping

Navigate to any shopping website's product page (Amazon, Walmart, Etsy, etc.). Click the Carbon0 extension icon in the toolbar, then click "Analyze Product" to see the carbon footprint and eco-friendly alternatives.

Pro Tip

Pin the Carbon0 extension to your toolbar for quick access to your shopping carbon footprint.
